About Me


MARIA LYN LORENZO was born in Malolos Bulacan on January 24, 1996.
She is a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering graduate at Bulacan State University and Kabataang Iskolar ni Del Pilar (KID) for 5 years.
For two decades, she chose to be different in a world that only Change is Permanent.

She has a great personality and a very talented person who knows how to make someone smile. She is trustworthy and a kind of person who will put others before herself. She is also generous and always creating something new everyday!.

At the age of 12, she was exposed on social media world. She loves the idea and the design of it and became interested in web designing. She was a college sophomore when she learn how to program ("Hello World"). Her knowledge and interest in software and web development has grown in recent years.

SHE HAS NO SPECIAL TALENT...SHE JUST PASSIONATELY CURIOUS.
